2. Phase 1: Full Syllabus Coverage Plan (27 May - 9 Jul)

This plan distributes the newly expanded detailed syllabus into the original 7-week timeframe[cite: 3]. Note: English, GK, and Reasoning should be practiced for 1-2 hours every single day.

Week 1: Fundamentals of Accounting
27 May - 2 Jun Accounting I: Principles, Journal, Ledger, Trial balance, Final accounts & Adjustments.
Week 2: Adv. Accounting & Banking Basics
3 - 9 Jun Accounting II: Depreciation, Bills of exchange, Co-op accounting, BRS.
Banking I: Scheduled/Non-Scheduled, RBI functions, CRR/SLR, NABARD, Banker-Customer relation, KYC.
Week 3: Costing & Core Banking Rules
10 - 16 Jun Accounting III: Cost accounting, Material control, Labour cost.
Banking II: NI Act 1881, Cheques, Crossings, All 6 Endorsements, Payment Systems (RTGS, NEFT, UPI).
Week 4: Financial Management & Deposits
17 - 23 Jun Accounting IV: Financial Planning, Ratios, Cash Flow, Fraud, Statutory Audit.
Banking III: Grievance (Ombudsman, COPRA), Deposits, Drafts, Lockers, Nomination, Lien, Set Off.
Week 5: Loans & Act/Rules Intro
24 - 30 Jun Banking IV: Priority Sector, KCC, MSME, SMA, NPA norms.
Act & Rules I: KCS Act 1969 - Registration, Membership, Share capital, Management.
Week 6: Act/Rules Completion & Principles Intro
1 - 7 Jul Act & Rules II: Surcharge (S.65), Audit (S.68), Arbitration (S.69), Disputes (S.70), Rules 1969.
Principles I: ICA, Foreign Co-op (Owen, Raiffeisen), Committees (VL Metha, CRAFICARD), PACS, PCARDB.
Week 7: Co-op Institutions & Mop-up
8 - 9 Jul Principles II: Urban, Housing, Marketing, Consumer, Dairy (APCOS), Industrial, SC/ST, Risk fund, Management, Dept Setup.
Review: Full mock tests, English vocab revision, Reasoning speed run.
